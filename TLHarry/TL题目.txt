Harry Potter :

To try and encourage more sales of the 5 different Harry
Potter books they sell, a bookshop has decided to offer
discounts of multiple-book purchases.

One copy of any of the five books costs 8 EUR.

If, however, you buy two different books, you get a 5%
discount on those two books.

If you buy 3 different books, you get a 10% discount.

If you buy 4 different books, you get a 20% discount.

If you go the whole hog, and buy all 5, you get a huge 25%
discount.

Note that if you buy, say, four books, of which 3 are
different titles, you get a 10% discount on the 3 that
form part of a set, but the fourth book still costs 8 EUR.

Your mission is to write a piece of code to calculate the
price of any conceivable shopping basket (containing only
Harry Potter books), giving as big a discount as possible.

For example, how much does this basket of books cost?

2 copies of the first book
2 copies of the second book
2 copies of the third book
1 copy of the fourth book
1 copy of the fifth book

Answer: 51.20 EUR


以前，有一套描述一个叫哈利的英国英雄的小说，共5本（在这个练习提出时，还只有5本），
让全世界的小朋友们都为之着迷，当然，出版商也是。

所以从提供优惠（同时提高销量）的角度出发，出版商提出了如下的折扣计算方法：

5本书中的任意一本标价8欧元。
如果你买了5本书中不同的两本，两本书都将有5%的优惠；
如果你买了3本不同的书，你将获得10%的优惠；
买了4本不同的数，将有20%的优惠；
如果你买了全套的5本书，你将有25%的优惠。
如果你买了4本书，其中3本是不同的，那么那三本不同的书将有10%的优惠，但重复的那一本则没有优惠。

现在的任务是写一段代码供书店来计算价格，给出尽可能大的折扣。

例如，对于以下的购书清单：
2本第一部。
2本第二部。
2本第三部。
1本第四部。
1本第五部。

答案是：51.20 EUR



题目要求：
1、功能要正确，单元测试设计合理，覆盖所有的场景
2、代码尽可能CC
3、相比算法实现的高效，我们更看重你的面向对象设计能力
